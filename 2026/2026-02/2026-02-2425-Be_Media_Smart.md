## Usage Tip

### So + be/aux. + S
Neither + be/aux. + S
You are tired. So am I.
He can't swim. Neither can I.
She loves reading detective novels, and so does her brother.
### By no means
By no means is this easy.
By no means/At no time should children be left alone at home without an adult watching.
