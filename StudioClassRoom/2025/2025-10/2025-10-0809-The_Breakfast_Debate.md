# The Breakfast Debate

Is breakfast necessary or just a habit?

by Linda Ludwig

Some people can't imagine starting their day without breakfast, while others are happy to run on empty until lunch. Some say it is necessary in order to stay energized and focused throughout the morning. Other people skip it without missing a beat.

Many studies show that eating breakfast is related to good health, **including** having a good memory and the ability to focus. Breakfast eaters lower their chance of high blood sugar levels and heart disease.

Some evidence suggests that heart attacks are more likely to happen in the morning. Dr. Francisco Lopez-Jimenez, a Mayo Clinic heart doctor says, "Skipping breakfast, historically, hasn't been good for your heart." He explains that part of the problem is the high adrenaline state that occurs in our bodies early in the morning. Without any food or calories, the body thinks it is **starving** and makes more adrenaline, creating a rush of the chemical. When this occurs **repeatedly**, people may experience various health problems.

When people do eat breakfast, many dieticians recommend including **protein** to keep their energy levels up and blood sugar low.

On the other side of the coin, others say that skipping breakfast won't harm your health. In fact, some research suggests that a pattern of eating and fasting, called intermittent fasting, is healthy. This method **involves** fasting for 16 hours and then eating during an eight-hour period. Many choose to include breakfast in their fasting time, which means the time they eat **extends** from lunch until dinner. This type of fasting has been shown to **effectively** reduce the number of calories a person eats and improves their metabolic health.

Other research shows that we need to not eat for a period of time. Intermittent fasting allows one's body to clean the waste left in it by dead and damaged cells. This in turn helps the body cut down on age-related diseases that may occur.

For those who feel they are overweight, reducing breakfast calories may help their energy balance improve. Additionally, skipping the first meal of the day may help people **reflect** on their relationship with food.

Breakfast can be important — but it's not a one-size-fits-all rule.

## Key Points
- Eating a healthy breakfast can support focus and heart health while skipping it may increase health risks.
- Skipping breakfast can be healthy for some people, especially when using intermittent fasting.

## Usage Tip
### Tip 1
be related to + N/V-ing

需要說明 A 與 B 兩者具有相關聯性時，可以用 A be related to B。Related 為形容詞，意為「有關聯的」，其後搭配 to 為介系詞，因此後方須接名詞 N 或動名詞 V-ing 來當 to 的受詞。動詞 associate（聯結）也可用來表達兩者相關聯，但其搭配是 A be associated with B。例句：Gender inequality is related to/associated with assigning traditional roles to men and women.

### Tip 2
S + has/have been shown to + V

本句型以現在完成式被動語態，表達「某事物已被證明會⋯⋯」。本句型能以主動語態呈現，如 Studies have shown that this type of fasting can effectively reduce ...，或以虛主詞 it 開頭，如 It has been shown that this type of fasting can effectively reduce ...。比起主動語態，使用被動語態和虛主詞 it 表達，能讓化消息來源，並更聚焦在被證明的事項上。例句：Social media addiction has been shown to increase levels of social anxiety.
