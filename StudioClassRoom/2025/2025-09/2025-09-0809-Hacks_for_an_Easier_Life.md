# Hacks for an Easier Life

Helpful hints that make your day-to-day life easier

by Ruth DeVine

Let's be real - life can pull you in all directions. Between school, friends and the never-ending call of social media, who doesn't feel like there's never enough time? Life hacks can help. They make performing everyday tasks easier, or more efficient, and save you time, effort or money.

If you're a student, do you study for hours without taking a break? Bad idea. Instead, study for 25 minutes, and then take a five-minute break. It keeps your brain fresh and focused. Don't put off your toughest task. Instead, start your day by completing it. Then you can enjoy the feeling of accomplishment all day. And if you hate reading your notes from class, record yourself reading them. Then listen while walking or before bedtime.

Do you struggle with spending less time on your phone? Resist the temptation by turning it to grayscale. Without bright colors, social media seems less fun. Also, discipline yourself to live without your phone for a brief stretch of time each day. Leave it in a drawer, another room or even zipped up in your backpack.

Broke again? Start small - save just a dollar a day. In a year, you'll have $365 without even thinking about it.

If you shop online, avoid buying anything on impulse. It is best to leave items in your cart for a few days, so you can determine if you really want them. Also, some stores will send you an email with a discount to get you to complete your purchase. Make a budget for your online shopping to give yourself a spending limit.

Taking care of mental health is also important. Write down your to-do list before bedtime. Then you can sleep like a baby without worrying about remembering what you have to do tomorrow. Also, jot down three things you're grateful for. That helps switch your focus from what's wrong in your life to what's right.

Finally, laugh more. Watch a funny video or hang out with people who make you laugh. Laughing releases stress and boosts your mood.

Use life hacks and see how life can get a little easier!

## Key Points

- Life hacks make performing everyday activities easier or more efficient.
- Laughing releases stress and boosts your mood.

## Usage Tip
### Tip 1
`Without N/V-ing, S + V`

介系詞 `without` 表「沒有」，其後接名詞 `N` 或動名詞 `V-ing`，可用來說明某一狀況缺乏或未發生時的情形。並說明事情會在何種情況下發生。例句：Without comparing the prices, you can't get a good deal.

### Tip 2
`switch from A to B`

動詞 `switch` 意為「從 A 換至 B」，`switch A for B` 亦常見，前者表「改變從 A 換至 B」，後者表「將 A 以 B 取代」。句中 `switch your focus from what's wrong to what's right` 表示將重心從負面轉到正面。例句：He exchanged/swapped his old phone for a newer model at the store.
