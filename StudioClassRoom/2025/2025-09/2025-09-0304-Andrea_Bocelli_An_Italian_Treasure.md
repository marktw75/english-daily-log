# Andrea Bocelli - An Italian Treasure

Learn more about one of the world's greatest singers

by Pamela Osment

Some people say that music soothes the savage beast. While it's true that music can calm people, it can also evoke intense emotions. Andrea Bocelli's beautiful singing certainly falls into the latter category. Considering the obstacles he has faced since birth, Bocelli's music and his life are an inspiration.

The famous singer was born in 1958 in the town of Lajatico, Italy. He developed severe vision problems at a young age, and at the age of 12, a soccer injury left him completely blind. Bocelli's condition, however, has not prevented him from living a full life. His talent has taken him from a small town to the international stage.

Born into a family of musicians, Bocelli exhibited an early talent for music. He learned to play the piano at age 6 and later the flute, trumpet, drums, guitar and more. His love for opera developed when, as a young boy, he listened and sang along to records of famous operas.

When Bocelli was about 12 and on holiday with his family, his uncle signed him up for a competition without asking him. That was the first time Bocelli performed in public. The audience loved him, and he won the event!

Though music was Bocelli's passion, his parents wanted him to pursue a legal career, so he earned a law degree. But he also continued singing and studying music.

It was in 1992 that Bocelli received his big break when someone passed one of his demo tapes to the world-famous tenor Luciano Pavarotti. Pavarotti was so impressed that he ended up recording "Miserere" with Bocelli - a dream come true for the unknown artist. The song became a smash in Europe, and Bocelli's star began to rise.

He became known for blending pop music and opera together, thereby acquiring the masses with opera. Throughout his career, the multi-award winner has performed in concerts around the world and sold millions of albums. He has performed with celebrities like Celine Dion and has sung before kings, presidents and other world leaders.

Over the years, his voice has touched millions, and his story has inspired just as many. Bocelli says that music is his life purpose, and he uses it to express emotions and spiritual feelings. He sees it as a gift from God. With his gifts and determination, the legendary singer has proven that anything is possible!

### A Family Christmas

Bocelli, who has sold over 80 million albums, released another one in 2022, but this one was very special. Two of his children, Matteo and Virginia, recorded the Christmas album, A Family Christmas, with him.

## Key Points

- Despite becoming completely blind, Andrea Bocelli never let that stop him from pursuing his dreams of becoming a famous singer.
- Andrea Bocelli's journey from studying law to inspiring people around the world shows his passion, hard work and talent for becoming a famous singer.

## Usage Tip
### Tip 1
`the latter`

`the former` / `latter` 指前文已提及兩項，前者是前者、後者是後者。句中 `the latter category` 即指前面提到的後者，也就是「能激起強烈情感」的那一類。例句：She was torn between studying literature and medicine; she pursued the latter but dedicated herself to writing years later.

### Tip 2
`It + be + NP/adv/PP ... that ...`

句型 `It is/was ... that ...` 可用於加強語氣，亦即所謂的分裂句。用來強調句子特定資訊時，會將原句中的內容拆開來再組合。句中的 `It was in 1992 that Bocelli received his big break`，強調的是時間 `in 1992`。例句：It is the experience of studying abroad that shapes his view of the world.
