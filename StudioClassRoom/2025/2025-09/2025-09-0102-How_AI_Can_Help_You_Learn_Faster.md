# How Artificial Intelligence (AI) Can Help You Learn Faster

Improve your English using AI

by Christine Bedwell

Learning English is easier with AI tools. These tools can help improve your vocabulary as well as your writing and speaking skills. Let's explore some of them.

It is necessary to have a broad vocabulary to communicate well in English. Quizlet lets you create vocabulary flash cards or use sets made by others. It also turns studying into a game, making learning fun and helping you remember words better. Quizlet is a great tool for reviewing vocabulary and helping you prepare for tests.

Writing well takes practice and editing, too! Write & Improve, created by Cambridge University, offers various writing tasks and provides instant feedback to help you improve. Additionally, a subscription to Cambridge's Test Zone provides practice questions and scores to help those preparing to take the IELTS. Grammarly is also an excellent writing tool. It can check your writing for grammar mistakes or errors in sentence construction. Then it suggests changes by identifying the mistakes and showing you how to edit them.

It takes diligent practice to become a confident reader. Microsoft Reading Coach uses passages at different levels to improve your reading speed and pronunciation. It records your reading and then identifies words that were pronounced wrong. You can listen to the correct pronunciation and practice until you get it right.

Speech calls for clear pronunciation. Check yours by recording your speech into SpeechTexter, which writes down what it hears to say. Paste this text and your written speech into DiffChecker to see where the two texts are different. This shows you which words you didn't pronounce correctly so you can practice. Then you can give a great presentation!

Does conversing in English make you nervous? You can practice without any stress by using SmallTalk2Me. It talks with you in English and helps you improve your speaking skills.

AI tools that help you expand your vocabulary, improve your writing and practice your speaking skills are valuable resources. Try them and see how rapidly your English improves!

## Key Points

- Communicate better by using artificial intelligence to memorize vocabulary, pronounce words correctly and converse more comfortably.
- Read rapidly with Microsoft Reading Coach, edit writing with Write & Improve or Grammarly and identify pronunciation mistakes through SpeechTexter.

## Usage Tip
### Tip 1
`It is adj. (for/of someone) to + V`

本句型以虛主詞 `It` 開頭，後方則以真正主詞 `to V` 回呼事件或動作的內容。前方需使用形容詞，`for + 某人`，意味著該形容詞是「對某人來說」；若是形容詞帶有「人的特質」意味時，則可搭配 `of + 某人`。前者較如技能、問題因人而異；後者與人的個性、品質或行為較有關，明顯因人而異。例句：It is wise of you to back up files regularly.

### Tip 2
`N + that/who/which/whose + N ...`

當關係代名詞／以助動詞 `N` 為其先行詞，並引出「形容詞子句」作為修飾語，用以補充說明 `N` 是什麼。若 `N` 為人，`which` 前可改為 `who`；若有代替的對象關係則用 `whose + N` 開頭。例句：The company whose CEO resigned last week is facing a management crisis.
