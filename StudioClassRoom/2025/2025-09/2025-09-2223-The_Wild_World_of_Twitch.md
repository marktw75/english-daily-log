# The Wild World of Twitch

The platform that changed the game

by David Morton

Imagine a place where you can watch people play video games, cook delicious meals or even chat about board games - all in real time! That describes Twitch, one of the biggest live-streaming platforms in the world.

Twitch was created by two college graduates who saw the power of live content. It began as part of Justin.tv, a streaming site launched in 2007 by two Yale graduates, Justin Kan and Emmett Shear. The platform covered a variety of topics, but gaming content quickly became the most popular. Seeing this demand, the creators launched a separate platform in June 2011 that focused entirely on gaming - Twitch.tv. Today, Twitch makes its content as available and diverse as it can. It hosts music performances, sports commentaries, art shows and even casual Just Chatting shows where streamers talk with their fans.

Seeing its potential, Amazon bought it for $970 million in 2014. Now Twitch has millions of streamers and over 31 million daily viewers. With its variety, it's like a giant digital concert, coffee shop and gaming stadium all in one.

One streamer who numbers in the top five for followers on Twitch as of March 2025 is Kai Cenat (aka KaiCenat). With an average of over 16.5 million followers, his streams are a blend of humor, storytelling and live discussions. Kai's genuine, down-to-earth personality even earned him an appearance on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon.

Félix Lengyel, popularly known as xQc, is one of the most-watched gaming streamers on Twitch. Starting his career as a professional gamer, xQc switched to full-time streaming where he showcases his gaming skills. His energetic commentary and authentic reactions have earned him over 12 million followers.

Imane Anys, known as Pokimane, is a streamer who has successfully blended gaming with lifestyle content. Pokimane has a following of over 9.3 million people and streams popular multiplayer games. In her Just Chatting broadcasts, she shares interesting thoughts and personal stories.

The streaming world is growing every day, and Twitch is the platform at the center of the action!

## Key Points

- Twitch is mostly known for its gaming content, but people use the platform to stream about a variety of topics.
- KaiCenat, xQc and Pokimane are popular streamers with millions of followers.

## Usage Tip
### Tip 1
`as + adj./adv. + as one can`

本句型可譯為「盡可能……」，用來表達方式或程度做到最大可能狀況。句中 `as available and diverse as it can` 即說明 Twitch 盡力讓內容更多元且更容易取得。例句：The candidate expressed his opinion as respectfully as he could, lest he offend others.

### Tip 2
`As of (time/date)`

`as of` 可連接某一時間點，表示「截至（某日期、某時間）」。常用來表示狀態在那個時點的情況。例句：As of next year, the rail fares will increase by 5 percent.
