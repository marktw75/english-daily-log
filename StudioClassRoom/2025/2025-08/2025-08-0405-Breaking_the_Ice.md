# Breaking the Ice

The new girl on the block

by Hazel Johansen

Cold winds blew across the snow and ice. But Tuxedo the penguin didn't notice. He was busy noticing something else.

"Who's that pretty penguin over there?" he asked his pal, Pen.

"I think she's new. Go over and break the ice," said his pal, Pen. "I can't," said Tuxedo. "You know I'm afraid of girls."

"I know, but just introduce yourself. You can do it," said Pen. "But what if I slip up?" asked Tuxedo.

"Just keep cool," said Pen, and he shoved Tuxedo, who slid right in front of the new girl. Tuxedo froze up.

"Oh!" said the startled little penguin. "Who are you?" she asked. "T-T-Tux," Tuxedo said. "N-N-Nice to meet you."

"I'm Penny," she smiled. "You're shaking. You must be freezing to death."

"Not really," explained Tuxedo. "I-I-I'm afraid."

"Of me?" Penny asked. "I'm af-f-fraid so," said Tuxedo sadly. "I'm af-f-fraid of almost everything."

"Well, let's go sliding together," encouraged Penny. "The ice is melting in places. We might break through," said Tux. "That's all right," reassured Penny. "We'll only slide where the ice is thick. Come on, let's go."

Just then an enormous, ugly wolf broke in. "This is my girl!" he growled. "Your girl?" said Penny. "I don't even know you."

"You will!" he exclaimed. "My girlfriend broke up with me. She broke my heart. But now I've found you!" he grinned. "I hate to break the news to you," she said. "But I'd never date a wolf like you."

The wolf growled angrily. "Nobody gives me the cold shoulder." He began dragging her across the ice. "Help!" she cried. "Tux, save me!"

Forgetting his fear, Tuxedo ran after the wolf and seized his ankle. Penny broke free. The wolf broke loose from Tuxedo, and Tuxedo backed away. "I can't believe you did that to me," the wolf snarled. "Don't you know you're skating on thin ice?"

"And you're standing on thin ice!" said Tuxedo. The wolf looked down and saw the ice around him cracking. Suddenly the block of ice that he was on broke off from the rest. "Arrr," cried the wolf as he drifted out to sea. "I've been left out in the cold."

Penny turned to Tuxedo. "You're my hero!" Tuxedo's heart melted. And the two slipped away hand in hand.

## Usage Tip
### Tip 1
`S + be busy + V-ing`

若要表達「忙於做某事」，形容詞 `busy`（忙碌的）後方可承接動名詞 `V-ing`。此外，`busy` 的後方也可承接「介系詞 `with` + 名詞 `N`」，表示在忙的事務，意近於 `be engaged in N`。上述 `busy` 的兩個用法大多不會混用，亦即通常不會出現 `busy` 後方承接 `with + V-ing` 的結構。例句：Angelina has recently been busy planning her wedding (= busy with/engaged in her wedding plans).

### Tip 2
`Just then` vs. `just now`

副詞 `just` 有「恰好」的意思。後方加上時間副詞，可以表達「就在（某時間點）」。如 `just then` 意為「就在那時」，用來指出過去某個特定時刻。`Just now` 則指出說話當下不久前的時間點，表「就在剛剛」，並且常用來修飾強調發生或持續時間不長的動作。例句：I called customer service just now, but no one answered.
