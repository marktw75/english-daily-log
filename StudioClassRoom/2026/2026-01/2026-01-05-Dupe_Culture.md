# Dupe Culture

Designer looks without luxury prices

Who says you have to spend a lot to look like a million dollars? With dupes, breaking the bank for popular items is a thing of the past.

Dupe culture, a popular buying trend, is changing the way people shop for designer fashion items, beauty products and more. Instead of buying famous brand products, people look for similar but less expensive alternatives.

"Dupes," which is short for "duplicates," are copies of high-end products. The most popular dupes are those that look almost the same as the originals but cost much less. They differ from counterfeit items, however. Counterfeits are passed off as the real thing, while people happy to disclose dupes as fakes.

For many shoppers, the thrill of discovering designer copies at bargain prices is an accomplishment that gives them bragging rights. Many then display their purchases on social media platforms like Instagram. This has helped add to the increasing popularity of dupe culture.

From beauty steals to fashion deals, dupe culture is turning everyone into smart shoppers!

## Key Point

Dupe culture is a buying trend that allows people to spend much less on items that resemble designer products.
