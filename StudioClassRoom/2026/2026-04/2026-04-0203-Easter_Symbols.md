# Easter Symbols

What do eggs, rabbits and chicks have to do with Easter?

by Pamela Osment

## Article

Happy Easter! This year, this religious holiday that also celebrates new life, falls on April 5. On Easter Sunday, Christians worldwide commemorate Jesus, the Son of God, rising from the dead. Let's look at a few symbols that are associated with the holiday.

### Eggs

The egg is an ancient symbol of new life. Just as a chick comes out of an egg, Christians see the egg as a symbol of Jesus rising from the tomb. Decorating eggs is a way to celebrate life and hope, a custom dating back to the 13th century.

### Rabbits

Christians adopted the rabbit as an Easter symbol after seeing the animal come out of its hole in the ground. To them, this was another symbol of Jesus coming out of the tomb and of new life.

### Lambs

At Easter, little lambs are also symbols of new life in spring and of Jesus.

### Lilies

Symbols of new life and hope, these white flowers bloom in the spring and are often called Easter lilies. People decorate homes and churches with them at Easter time.

## Key Points

- Eggs, rabbits, lambs and lilies are all important symbols of Easter.

## Usage Tip

### Tip 1
S + V, N + V-ing

句型是指分詞構句用法，用來說明補充說明主句所發生的事情。若主詞相同，無須明說第二個動作的主詞。The meeting ended peacefully, the outcome pleasing everyone involved. 會議平和地結束，結果令所有參與者滿意。這個句構有豐富的語意表達，可說明時間背景、原因背景、伴隨狀態、方式、結果等。例如：She stood in silence, a woman overwhelmed by sorrow. 她靜靜地站著，是一位被悲傷淹沒的女人。

### Tip 2
come out

可譯作意思是「出來」，表示「從裡面到外面」的動作；也可指「結果證明」、「出現了 The new book will come out next month. 新書將於下個月上市。也可譯作「說，真相被揭露」。如：The truth came out. 真相被揭露。此外，還能表示「由無變有」。如：The sun came out. 太陽出來了。Come out 也有「露面」的意思，例如：We waited for the famous actress to come out of the theater. 我們等候那位著名女演員從劇院出來。
