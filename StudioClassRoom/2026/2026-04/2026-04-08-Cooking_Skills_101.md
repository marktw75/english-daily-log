# Cooking Skills 101

Grab your apron and get ready for a delicious adventure!

by Pamela Osment

## Article

Perhaps you've watched some of your favorite TV chefs and thought, "I wish I could cook like that!" Odds are you can, with a few basic cooking and kitchen-safety tips.

For starters:

- Always wash your hands well before handling any food.
- Keep raw and cooked food separate.
- Learn to handle knives in a safe manner.
- Learn how to slice meat and vegetables evenly. Watching YouTube videos will teach you.
- Become familiar with kitchen appliances and tools.
- Learn which tools to use to measure wet and dry ingredients.
- Read recipes twice. Then gather all the ingredients before beginning to cook.
- Learn how to boil water, and you'll be ready to boil eggs and to cook pasta, vegetables and more.
- Learn how to cook rice. It forms the basis for many meals.
- Learn to stir-fry. You can combine meat and vegetables to make a quick and healthy meal.
- Learn how to make basic sauces to add to meat, vegetables and stir-fried dishes.

Mastering fundamental cooking skills is the key to healthy eating. From boiling noodles to cooking rice, every great chef starts with the basics.

## Usage Tip

### Tip 1
S + wish + S + V-ed

用來表達「現在在某實際狀況的願望」。主詞後的 wish 表示「希望」，後面接虛擬語氣的從句，從句通常使用過去式來表達與現在現實相反的願望。例如：I wish I were taller so I could play basketball better. 我希望自己能高一點，這樣就能打籃球打得更好。

### Tip 2
Odds are that ...

相當於 it is likely that ... 或 chances are that ...。Odds 原意是「賠率，可能性」，當放在句首使用時，通常用來表達說話者對某件事發生機率的預測。若表示不太可能，則可說 odds are against in favor of + N/V-ing。例如：Odds are you'll miss the train if you don't leave right now. 如果你現在不出發，很可能會搭不上火車。
