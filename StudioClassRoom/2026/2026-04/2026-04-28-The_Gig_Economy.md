# The Gig Economy

The new normal way to work

by Linda Ludwig

## Article

The future is here! What was once thought of as an alternative way to work is now the new normal. More and more workers move from one gig to the next instead of staying with one long-term employer. From independence and extra cash to flexibility and creativity, the gig economy is pulling people away from traditional work schedules.

Temporary, contract and freelance jobs characterize the gig job market. People provide on-demand work, services or goods to earn some income. The digital age of apps has enabled this shift. For example, in the Philippines, ride apps like Grab and Angkas Padala allow daytime drivers to become evening food-delivery people. In South Korea, Coupang helps people generate income by delivering packages on their own schedules.

Some downsides for gig workers include a lack of health benefits, paid leave and job security. For businesses, missed deadlines, work quality that isn't consistent and reliable workers are major concerns.

Even though these problems exist, the trend is growing. In fact, a study found that nearly 40 percent of people born between 1997 and 2012 (Gen Z) are engaged in gig work.

Side hustles, like selling food from a temporary stand after finishing one's day job, fit the gig definition too. Also included is selling jewelry, clothing and even organic products on social media sites, effectively converting them into marketplaces.

In 2024, estimates showed that over 1.5 billion people were engaged in gig work. A further projection is that more than half of Southeast Asian urban workers could be participating in gig work by 2030. Trends show that the gig economy is here to stay.

## Key Point

- From traditional jobs to today's job market, the gig economy has become a major part as people move to temporary, freelance or on-demand work.

## Usage Tip

### Tip 1
What was once ... + be + now ...

意思是「曾經是……的現在卻變成……」。例如：What was once a small fishing village is now a modern tourist city. 過去曾經是一個小漁村，如今成了一座現代化觀光城市。

### Tip 2
engage(d) in + N/V-ing

表示「從事；參與」。在詞尾 in 通常用來表示投入某個活動或領域。例句：This company is engaged in the development of sustainable energy sources. 這家公司正投入於永續能源的開發。
