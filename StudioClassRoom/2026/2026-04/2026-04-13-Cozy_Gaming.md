# Cozy Gaming

Power up ... and slow down

by Heather Lin

## Article

Imagine playing video games that feel like a warm blanket on a rainy afternoon. Unlike some games in which playing at a fast pace reigns supreme, cozy games take a gentler approach. Their focus is relaxation. Players set their own speed and make creative choices without levels or the pressure to "win."

In these games, you grow plants, cook, decorate or build as well as make friends with delightful characters. The music is soft and the colors are warm.

Cozy games such as The Sims, Harvest Moon and Animal Crossing have been popular for years. But they became even more so during the pandemic. The various games helped people connect when meeting in person wasn't possible. Since the pandemic, cozy games have remained popular because they help people relax.

Cozy games remind us that it's OK to slow down and smell the roses. They offer peaceful entertainment. They also give people an opportunity for a break in a busy world.

Whenever you want to release some stress, try out a cozy game. It may be just the gentle escape you need.

## Key Point

- Cozy games create a peaceful space where players can enjoy life's simple pleasures.

## Usage Tip

### Tip 1
reign supreme

字面上的意思是「至尊無上地統治」，reign 是「統治」之意，supreme 則表示「最高的」。Reign supreme 常用來表示某人、事物、觀念或趨勢在某段時間內最具主導地位。例如：For decades, the company has reigned supreme in the smartphone market. 這家公司在智慧型手機市場上稱霸數十年。

### Tip 2
S + become + (even) more so ...

表示「某種情況變得更加如此」，其中 so 代指前面提到的形容詞或描述，避免重複使用 more 來加強程度，而在比較級中可加上 even 來強化。例如：Tracy is usually quiet, but she became even more so during the difficult period after her move. Tracy 平常就很安靜，但在搬家後的那段艱難時期，她變得更加沉默。
