# The Benefits of Sparkling Water

Those bubbles aren't just fun. They're doing your body a favor

by Pamela Osment

## Article

Who knew tiny bubbles could pack such a punch? Sparkling water, also known as soda water or carbonated water, isn't just fun to drink, it's full of benefits, too.

Added sugars and artificial colors found in many drinks can be harmful. Yet research has shown that sparkling water without them can be healthy.

Refreshing carbonated water, many people's preferred choice over plain water, helps keep the body hydrated. This, in turn, supports healthy skin, good concentration, heart health, muscle function and more.

Studies have shown that drinking sparkling water aids in swallowing and digesting food. It's also thought to give a full feeling, which helps with weight management.

For years, people have believed that carbonated water harms bones. Yet, to date, no evidence supports that theory. To the contrary, research suggests carbonated water may actually improve bone health.

Keeping all these advantages in mind, many people buy machines to make carbonated water at home. Healthy additions like fruit can be added to make custom waters.

From improved concentration to weight management, sparkling water proves that bubbles really can make your life better.

## Key Point

- Sparkling water, without harmful additions, can benefit the body in many ways.

## Usage Tip

### Tip 1
to the contrary

意思是「與此相反」，用於表示說話者提出相反的觀點或證據。在台灣的新聞或評論文章裡，有時也會翻成相反地。前面通常會先提到一種觀點，接著再用 to the contrary 強調後面的對比。例如：Many people thought the plan would fail, but the results proved to the contrary. 許多人以為計畫會失敗，但結果證明剛好相反。
