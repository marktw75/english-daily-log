# Randy Travis

A music legend overcomes a medical crisis and returns to the stage

by Elizabeth Koroma

## Article

Randy Travis ranked as one of country music's most popular artists until a stroke nearly killed him in 2013. The stroke left him with difficulty moving and speaking, which most people would have expected to put an end to his career. But 12 years after this medical crisis, Travis went on tour once again. He even released a new song with the help of AI, which makes his comeback even more amazing.

Born in 1959, Travis began trying to become a professional musician in the early 1980s. Most record companies at the time were looking for country artists whose style resembled pop music. Since Travis wanted to perform traditional country music, it took him almost four years to get a record deal. But when a studio finally agreed to release his first album, it discovered that his style was far from a liability. Fans loved Travis and praised him for saving country music by returning it to its roots.

Travis released several albums that focused on specific themes, which was not a common choice. For example, his album Heroes and Friends honored people who had influenced him. Travis also engaged in some acting and raised money for various charities.

But in 2013, disaster struck as Travis suffered a stroke. Three days earlier, he had been hospitalized for heart failure, and the stroke was related to his heart problems. The doctors said that he was on the verge of death with less than a two percent chance of surviving. And when he developed a respiratory infection on top of everything, they encouraged his wife, Mary, to take him off life support. But when Mary went over to her husband, he squeezed her hand and shed a tear, and she knew he wanted to keep fighting to survive.

Travis did recover, but he had to learn how to walk again, and he still struggles to speak. And yet, he has been able to go on tour, where he greets fans, and his band plays his songs. He even released a music video. Although Travis could not sing the song himself, he had AI use recordings of his earlier songs to make a video with his voice singing the new song. Travis stands out as an example of someone who overcame great challenges through determination and love of life.

## Key Points

- Randy Travis went against the trend at the time by performing traditional country music and became popular.
- Travis survived a stroke and recovered enough to go on tour and use AI to complete a new song.

## Usage Tip

### Tip 1
S + ranked as one of + N + ranks + 某人/事被列為……之一

其中 ranked 是動詞 rank（排名、列入等級）的過去分詞，用來表達評比後的結果。介系詞 as 表示「被認為」，one of + N 則由片語來表達「某人／事在某類別中名列前茅」。例如：Tajpei ranks as one of the safest cities in the world. 台北被列為世界上最安全的城市之一。

### Tip 2
less than + a + (number) + percent chance

這個表達可用來表示某事發生機率很低。例如：There is less than a five percent chance of it raining tomorrow. 明天下雨的機率不到百分之五。
